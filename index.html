About Me

I am Mahesh Sharma, an educator with 21 years of experience in teaching academic subjects and 18 years of expertise in Abacus and Vedic Maths.
Throughout my career, I have taught and mentored over 3000 students, helping them build strong fundamentals and a love for mathematics.
My teaching approach is practical, interactive, and tailored to each student's needs, ensuring they gain confidence and excel in their studies.
I am passionate about making learning enjoyable and accessible for all, and I strive to empower every student to achieve their academic goals.
